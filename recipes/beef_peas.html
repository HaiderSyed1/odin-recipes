Asian Beef with Snow Peas

Home

Description

This is probably the simplest stir fry someone could make. Minimal ingredients, and only a couple minuites of prep work. Easy for weekday meals. You know the vibes


ingredients

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice wine
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp veg oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1 pound beef round steak
  • 8 ounces of snow peas

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, rice wine, brown sugar and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or skillet over medium high heat. Stir-fry ginger and garlic for 30 seconds. Add the steak and stir-fry for 2 minutes or until evenly browned.
  3. Add the snow peas and stir-fry for an additional 3 minutes. Add the soy sauce mixture, bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
  4. Lower heat and simmer until the sauce is thick and smooth. Serve immediately.
